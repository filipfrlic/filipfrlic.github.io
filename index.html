About Me

With over 4 years of specialized experience in React Native development, I create sophisticated mobile applications that combine exceptional performance with intuitive user experiences. I focus on developing cross-platform solutions that maintain native-quality functionality for both iOS and Android platforms.

My technical approach emphasizes architecture-driven development, implementing clean, modular code structures that scale efficiently. I leverage advanced state management solutions including Redux Toolkit, MobX, and Zustand to handle complex application states, while implementing cutting-edge animations using Reanimated and Skia to create fluid, responsive interfaces that delight users.

I excel at integrating RESTful APIs, third-party services, and implementing complex mapping solutions with Google Maps and Mapbox. My experience includes complete application lifecycle management—from initial concept and architecture design through deployment, utilizing modern CI/CD pipelines to ensure consistent quality and reliable releases.
